About dataset

The Indeed dataset offers a complete overview of all job postings in the US and the UK listed on Indeed, encompassing all the data you need for competitive intelligence and to make informed hiring-related decisions.
The dataset includes all major data points:

  • Position
  • Company
  • Description
  • Location
  • Salary
  • Publish date
  • Company rating
  • and more

Frequently asked questions

The price of the dataset depends on the complexity of obtaining the data, the added information (enrichment), and the number of records. Pricing for datasets varies, and customized subsets can be purchased at a lower rate.

Most of our datasets are updated on a monthly basis. The “timestamp” field in each record indicated when it was collected, you can use the timestamp filter to limit results to the requested period.

Records include urls to images. In some cases the images themselves can be acquired as separate datasets

Datasets have a minimum price that covers a certain amount of records. When creating a subset of the dataset, the value is not from the # of results but from the ability to query the entire dataset and to narrow the results so they can answer specific business questions.

We haven't implemented yet an API but one is planned in the near future

Yes, we can provide larger samples, please contact our sales representatives

We can perform queries that combine two different datasets, assuming there is common identifier in both datasets we can use as reference, please contact our data experts if you have such need

When we refresh a dataset, we store some or all of its past values, for most dataset we have up to 1 year of historical data.
